Summary

My Novel Garden is a gardening app that monitors the light and moisture levels for your garden.

The following technologies will be used:

  • Arduino
  • Sparkfun
  • Angular
  • Express

API

The Novel Garden will have it's own API with Garden Tips.

Other

Stretch: The app will be connected to a watersource and LED panel to supplement plants when the levels are low.
